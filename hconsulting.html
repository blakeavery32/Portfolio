Finding High Value Targets

I will use prescription data to determine which physicians are prescribing your drug the most and rank every physician from the top tenth (decile 10) to the bottom tenth (decile 1).

Analyzing Brand Value

I will use prescription data to inform you which of your medications is being prescribed the most, which medications sell well together, and how well each of your medication's sales are growing.

Developing Call Strategy

Once I know who prescribes your medication the most, I will research prescriptions per visit to determine how often your reps should visit each physician and create a call plan for each of your reps.


Sizing Your Sales Force

By knowing how many visits each sales rep can make and should be making and how many physicians are in the country, I will optimally size your workforce to maximize profit and minimize cost.

Aligning Your Sales Force

By researching where physicians who are highly valuable to your firm are located, I will properly align your territories to ensure every sales rep has the ability to visit every high value physician in their new territory. I will also ensure sales reps do not have a wasted territory where no high value physicians are located.
